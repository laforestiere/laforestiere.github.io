Our bed and breakfast

Discovering Alsace while staying in a warm, confortable and convivial house situated accross hiking trails for an active or relaxing stay surrounded by nature

Our house is located at the end of a private trail, in a touristic mountain village, 8 km from the Alsace wine route and 40 minutes away from Strasbourg and Colmar.

Five guest bedrooms with very high comfort, peaceful, of wich two are family suites, decorated with refined materials like oak solid wood floorboard and Slate on the floor, lime plasters, King Size high quality bedding, sound insulation between bedrooms.

A great landscaped garden with terraces where breakfast will be served as soon as the weather permits, and a spa jacuzzi outdoor for a relaxing and cocooning stay.

Outdoor activities

From our house:

Hiking: our village is undoubtedly the perfect place for hikers of all levels, kilometers of waymarked hiking trails, Inns to refill yourself, picturesque landscape, itinerary at you disposal: en route !

Mountain Biking: waymarked trails starting from our house, for every level, maps, itinerary at your disposal as well as our garage and garden pipe to wash bikes, we can also book a guide, everything is simplified for bikers.

E-bike or Electric bicycle can be easily praticed from our house with maps and adapted route, they can also be rented not far from us

5 minutes from home:

Tennis course at your disposal for free in the village
Fitness trail 2,3 km long in the village

10 minutes from home:

Champ du Feu ski resort at 1100 m in altitude with 14 alpin trails and more than 50 km of cross-country and skating skiing, snowhoeing, walking, sledding. There is also a ski school.
